Preserve and Promote African Languages

Site Name

Preserve and Promote African Languages

This name directly represents the mission of the site, which is to protect and celebrate the diverse languages of Africa. It is clear, descriptive, and immediately conveys the site's purpose.

Optional domain availability: preserve-african-languages.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide a comprehensive resource for learning about and preserving African languages. It offers information about various African languages, educational resources, and community engagement opportunities to support language preservation efforts.

Scenarios

  • What resources are available for learning Yoruba?
  • How can I connect with community groups dedicated to preserving African languages?
  • Are there any success stories of language preservation efforts that I can learn from?
  • Where can I find online courses or books to learn African languages?

Color Schema

Green (#006400): Used for the header, footer, buttons, and accents to symbolize growth and renewal, reflecting the mission of preserving languages.

Light Gray (#f4f4f4): Used for the background of sections to provide a clean and readable contrast against the text.

Typography

Heading Font: Arial, sans-serif - Used for all headings to ensure readability and a modern look.

Body Font: Georgia, serif - Used for body text to provide a classic and easy-to-read feel.
